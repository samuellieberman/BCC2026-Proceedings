# Scratch Jr: Code, Create & Play

## Duane Erickson

### Boise Code Camp 2026 - May 2nd, 2026

Perfect for our youngest designers (ages 5 - 7), this hands-on session is all about fun, learning, and imagination! Together with a family member, campers will be introduced to basic coding concepts through playful, interactive games. They'll design unique backgrounds, bring charming characters to life, and discover the endless possibilities of game design. No experience needed!
