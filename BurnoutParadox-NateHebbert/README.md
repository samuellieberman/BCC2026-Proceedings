# The Burnout Paradox

## Nate Hebbert

### Boise Code Camp 2026 - May 2nd, 2026

People often burn out not because they are doing too much, but because they aren’t doing enough of the activities that give them life. This is what I call the “burnout paradox”.

This workshop helps participants understand their own needs, identify activities that fill those needs, and learn how to make those activities non-negotiable in their lives.

I believe understanding the burnout paradox was the single greatest factor in recovering my ability to work after my own case of severe burnout.
