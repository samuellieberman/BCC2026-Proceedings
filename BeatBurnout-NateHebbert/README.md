# How To Beat Burnout Without Pressing Pause

## Nate Hebbert

### Boise Code Camp 2026 - May 2nd, 2026

Format: Workshop

A lot of people think you have to slow down—or even completely stop—to beat burnout. But the more I’ve learned, the more I’ve realized how many ways there are to manage burnout without pressing pause.

In this workshop, I go over the seven controls of energy management with live coaching for participants (to demonstrate those controls).

1. Subtraction — Removing everything that unnecessarily drains energy 
2. Addition — Adding energizers to your life
3. Optimization — Finding ways to optimize everything you do
4. Structure — Organizing and planning
5. Balance — Managing energy in real time
6. Alignment — Bringing your life into sync with your unique needs, capacities, and values
7. Growth — Increasing your resilience and capacities
